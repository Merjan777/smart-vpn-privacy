Privacy Policy for Smart VPN

Last updated: [16.12.2025]

Smart VPN ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.
This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

Smart VPN does NOT collect or store:
- Browsing history
- DNS queries
- IP addresses
- Network traffic content
- Personally identifiable information

2. VPN Service

Smart VPN functions as a VPN service and routes network traffic securely.
We do not log or monitor user activity.

3. App Usage Data

To support features such as "Auto VPN per App", Smart VPN may detect:
- Which application is currently in the foreground

This information:
- Is processed locally on the device
- Is NOT stored
- Is NOT transmitted to any server

4. Advertising

Smart VPN uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob).
AdMob may collect limited data such as:
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- Approximate location (non-precise)

This data is handled according to Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

Smart VPN may use:
- Google AdMob
- Google Play Services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data.
No sensitive or personal data is stored on our servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Smart VPN does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
Email: smartvpn.support@gmail.com
